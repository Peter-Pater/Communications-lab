Player

The best player in the club history

The Frenchman had exquisite technique, searing pace and surprising strength. And like the typical
schoolboy superstar, he did pretty much everything for his team. Henry was the captain, the talisman,
the dead-ball expert, the penalty-taker and the assist-maker.
Then there's the goals, a club record 228 of them to be precise. And what a portfolio Henry built
up during his eight years as Arsenal. Thumping drives, delicate chips, jinking runs, audacious backheels,
even the odd header - the Frenchman had myriad ways to find the net.
